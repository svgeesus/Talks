Conclusions

What can we learn from this

  • Be aware of past efforts
  • Prototype, test, iterate
  • Fail early, fail often
  • Find out what is  really  missing
  • Check feasibility at scale
  • Ensure multi-vendor buy-in
  • Active community (polyfils, bug reporting)
  • Work in public, encourage participation
